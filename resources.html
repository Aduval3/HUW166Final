Essentials for Aspiring Animators

Adobe Animate CC

Animate CC is a long-running animation program that went under many changes over the decade. It is still widely used by freelance animators and is most popularly used in Sprite Animation.

Link

Last updated 3 mins ago

Clip Studio Paint

Clip Studio Paint is a drawing program that gives you the ability to animate. Unlike Flash, this program is more catered to hand-drawn animators with it's many different brush styles and quality options.

Link

Last updated 3 mins ago

Wacom Tablet

Animation these days are completely digital. With the exception of a few traditional studios. Drawing tablets are the best for making sure you get the perfect strokes on your animating program. Mostly used in Clip Studio Paint.
